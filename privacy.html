Privacy Policy for VibeCheck

Effective Date: July 23, 2026

VibeCheck is an open-source web application designed to help users view their personal Spotify statistics, live status, and generate custom playlists.

Data We Use

VibeCheck uses the official Spotify Web API to access your top artists, top tracks, and your currently playing song. This allows the app to generate your statistics and build a playlist on your behalf.

Data Storage & Sharing

We do not store, save, or share any of your personal data. Everything happens locally in your web browser. No databases are used to collect user information, and no data is sent to third parties.

Revoking Access

You can completely revoke VibeCheck's access to your Spotify account at any time. Simply visit your Spotify Account Settings, find "VibeCheck" in the list, and click "Remove Access".
